# House Chores

## Daily Tasks

- Make the bed.

- Do the dishes – or at least put them in the dishwasher.

- Clean the kitchen sink.

- Wipe tables and kitchen countertops.

- Empty the trash if it's full.

## Weekly Tasks

### Every Week

- Do the laundry.

- Vacuum.

### Week 1

- Dust – especially surfaces that collect a lot of dust.

- Wash the floors.

### Week 2

- Change bed linens.

- Clean the bathroom – sink, toilet, mirrors, etc.

## Monthly Tasks

- Wash windows.

- Clean inside the refrigerator and discard old items.

- Clean the stove and range hood.
